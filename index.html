Micro and Macro Jams are Game Jams (game development competition) held on itch.io. Every Jam has a pre-set theme that is divulged once the Jam starts. The prerequisite can be voted on in the Micro Jam Discord server and is open for interpretation.

It's absolutely fine if you do not finish your game in time. You can submit it late anytime within the Jam's voting period!

-THE GAME JAMS-

Micro Jam

A bi-weeky jam

Duration: 48 hours (2 days) , usually on weekends

Micro Jam is a bi-weekly Game Jam (game development competition) held on itch.io. Every Jam has a pre-set theme and a surprise prerequisite that is divulged once the Jam starts. The prerequisite can be voted on in the Micro Jam Discord server and is open for interpretation.

Find One

Macro Jam

A monthly jam

Duration: 96 hours (3 days) , usually on weekends

Macro Jam is a beginner friendly Game Jam that lasts 96-hours and is held on the first week of every month. In this Jam you have to create a game that follows the theme, which will be revealed once the Jam starts. The theme can be voted and suggested in the Discord Server. During the submission window you can play and vote other peoples' games, and 3 days after, the rankings will be out.
